About OG Card Generator

Extension information and version details

OG Card Generator is a powerful Chrome extension that fetches Open Graph metadata from websites and transforms it into beautifully formatted preview cards perfect for email sharing. The extension features intelligent image selection with a sophisticated scoring algorithm, extensive customization options including colors, fonts, borders, and typography, as well as card history persistence and debug mode for developers. Whether you're sharing links in professional emails or personal messages, OG Card Generator makes your content stand out with polished, professional-looking cards.

Version: 3.2.0
